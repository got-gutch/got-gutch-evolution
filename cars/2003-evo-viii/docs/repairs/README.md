## FUTURE: Front Lower Control Arm (LCA) Bushing Refresh — Powerflex Purple (Street)

**Status:** Planned  
**Technician:** Eli at Tuned Up LLC  

| Component | Part Number | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| LCA Rear Bushing (Large) | PFF44-202G | **Highly Recommended.** Caster Adjustable / Anti-Lift Kit. This is the definitive cure for the "metallic bang" under braking. Replaces weak, oil-filled factory rubber with dual-material polyurethane that cannot bottom out. Adds Anti-Lift geometry to reduce brake dive and positive caster for improved steering feel and high-speed stability. Makes the car feel 5 years newer. |
| LCA Front Bushing (Small) | PFF44-1011-14 | Controls toe-alignment stability during hard cornering. **Verify 14mm bolt size** — earlier Evo 4/5/6 used 12mm; 14mm is standard for Evo 8/9. |
| Front Sway Bar (Optional) | PFF44-201-24 | 24mm is stock Evo 8 size. Cheap to do while the car is up. |

**Why This Fixes the "Bang":**  
The PFF44-202G kit physically cannot bottom out like the stock rubber does when it's torn. Under braking, the control arm stays exactly where it belongs, and brake pedal feel becomes much more consistent because the wheel isn't moving backward anymore.

**Recommended Action During Service:**  
With control arms off to press in bushings, have Eli inspect the lower ball joints. If original, they are likely equally worn and should be replaced at the same time.
